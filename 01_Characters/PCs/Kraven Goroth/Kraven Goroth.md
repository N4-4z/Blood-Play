## 🛠️ Skills and Abilities

### Skills
*Usable once per long rest*
1. Emperor's Throne- Creates a bone throne for the user to sit on while seated all magical and physical attacks are halved(after subtracting R) and the user is incapacitated until concentration breaks or he dies, he deals 2x PC level to all enemies in a 25ft radius and also takes PC level worth of dmg while its active.
2. Arise- You can raise any creature (as long as u can match its DC (DC will be 5+ MP it cost's to sustain said creature) until they  are decapacitated or willed out of existence.
3. Emperor's Fortitude- You can replace any saving throw with a NAT 20.

### Abilities[6]
*Usable Class Level times per day*
1. **Spikes**- Bone spikes are raised from the ground underneath the enemies feet making the terrain in a 25 ft radius difficult to traverse and if they fail a DC 13 Reflex save they take PC level piercing dmg.
2. **Bone Cage** - A bone cage is formed around a creature/ a group of creatures(limited to 15ft) requires a  DC 17 STR saving throw or DC 17  Reflex to dodge.
3. **Hand Of the Fallen** - Creates a massive bone hand that can be used as a wall is weak to bludgeoning dmg and takes a DC 15 STR to break (DC 10 for bludgeoning dmg) can deal (1d4 x PC level ) bludgeoning dmg has dimensions 25ft , 5ft
